MockupGo simplify how you get approval on your (responsive) web design mockups

MockupGo is an online tool that makes it quick and easy to present, share and get feedback on all your design mockups, whether static images or responsive html prototypes. It will help you show clear and beautiful presentation to your client or team and iterate faster on your design.

  1. upload your image or html mockups.

    Multiple images for different screen sizes, or directly responsive html. Everything is hosted for you.

  2. send a link

    Give access to your client through an unguessable link (no password required).

  3. let your client review your work.

    Wether he reviews it from a desktop or a mobile device, he will always have an accurate preview.

  4. receive feedbacks.

    MockupGo is easy to use for your clients: they can give feedback by annotating and drawing directly on the mockups.
