Simplifies client approval and feedback on web design mockups

MockupGo is an online tool that makes it quick and easy to share and get feedback for both static images and responsive html prototypes. It provides on-page feedback for your team or client, and supports faster iterations of your design.

  1. upload your images or html mockups.

    We host everything for you, and do not limit the amount of images or files.

  2. send the link to your client or team

    A unique link is generated that only you and whomever you share it with will know. No password required.

  3. your work can be reviewed on any device

    Wether he reviews it from a desktop or a mobile device, he will always have an accurate preview.

  4. receive feedbacks.

    MockupGo is easy to use for your clients: they can give feedback by annotating and drawing directly on the mockups.
